Welcome to my blog!

I plan on posting some HackTheBox writeups soon, and eventually some other stuff too. In the main page you can find a reel of the 6 most recent HTB writeups as well as the 6 most recent blog posts.

As far as the HTB writeups are concerned, right now they are designed to be encrypted if the machine is active, and unencrypted when they retire. I have a cronjob that will check every day to see if any of the active machines retire. When this happens, those retired posts will get moved to the front of the reel for ~30 days and will be unencrypted. To read the encrypted articles, you'll have to go fetch a 32-character value from the related machine. Don't worry, I tell you exactly what to grab, you'll see.

I don't really intend on posting anything non-technical here, with the exception of this first post :)
